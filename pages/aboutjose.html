about jose rizal

Jose Rizal was a Filipino nationalist, writer, and revolutionary who is considered a national hero in the Philippines. Jose Rizal’s martyrdom and writings inspired the Philippines Revolution against Spain. He is revered as a symbol of Filipino nationalism, patriotism, and intellectual excellence. His execution fueled the flames of the revolution that eventually led to independence of the Philippines from Spanish colonial rule.

Full Name José Protasio Rizal Mercado y Alonzo Realonda
Born June 19, 1861, Around 11pm to 12 am
Place of Birth Calamba, Laguna, Philippines
Height 5 feet and 3 inches (160 cm) tall.
Racial Origin Filipino, Malay, Chinese and Spanish
Died December 30, 1896 (aged 35)
Founder Liga Filipina

Attire

Late 1800s. As more of the prinicipalia and the ilustrados went abroad, they were exposed to the fashion trends in Europe, which is why Rizal has an excellent sense of style and is often dressed in European fashion at the time. Depending on the occasion, he was often seen dressed in a suit or traditional Philippine clothing. Simple, well tailored Western suits in colors such as white, natural, and black were replaced by colorful jackets with checkered patterns.
