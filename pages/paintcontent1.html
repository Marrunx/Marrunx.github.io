Rizal Works (Paintings)

Jose Rizal, a national hero of the Philippines, achieved remarkable feats in various fields. Here are some of his notable 8 paintings that he made.


  • Saturnina Rizal

    Title: Saturnina Rizal
    Material: Oil
    Remarks: Now in Rizal Shrine in Fort Santiago

  • Dapitan Church Curtains

    Title: Dapitan church curtains
    Material: Oil
    Remarks: Made in Dapitan, 1894


  • A Painting on a pair of mother-of-pearl

    Title: A painting on a pair of mother-of-pearl
    Material: Oil
    Remarks: Shells painted by Rizal in Dapitan and given as a gift to Doña Leonor Valenzuela and later passed into the hands of Doña Margarita Valenzuela


  • Spanish Coat of Arms

    Title: Spanish coat of arms Material: Water color
    Remarks:
    Done during a fiesta of San Rafael in Calamba in 1867


  • Allegory on a pair of Porcelain Bases of the New Year Celebration (1886)

    Title: Allegory on a pair of porcelain bases of the new year celebration
    Material: Oil
    Remarks: Made in Berlin in 1886


  • Christ Crucified (1875)

    Title: Christ crucified
    Material: Crayon
    Remarks: 1875

  • Immaculate Conception (1874)

    Title: Immaculate Conception
    Material: Crayon
    Remarks: Made in Manila, 1974


  • Portrait of Morayta

    Title: Portrait of Morayta
    Material: Crayon
    Remarks: Made in Barcelona, 1885
